Sales Performance Dashboard Project

Project Overview

The Sales Performance Dashboard project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to understand overall business performance across regions, product categories, and time periods. The goal is to convert raw sales data into meaningful business insights and present them through clear and interactive dashboards.

Business Objectives

Analyze overall sales and profit performance
Identify top performing regions
Compare product category performance
Track monthly sales and profit trends
Support data-driven business decision making

Tools & Technologies Used

Excel – Data cleaning, pivot tables, and dashboard creation
SQL (MySQL) – Data analysis using queries
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard design

Dataset

Sample Superstore Sales Dataset
Contains order-level details such as region, category, sub-category, sales, quantity, and profit.
